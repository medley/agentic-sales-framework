# Generic Email Style Corpus

Use these as **fallback examples** when no user-specific email corpus exists.
They follow the patterns from `email_style_guide.md`.

Each example includes:
- Mode
- Subject
- Body

---

## 1. Cold Outbound (Example)

**Mode:** cold_outbound  

**Subject:** Exploring options to reduce batch record errors

Hi {{first_name}},

Noticed {{company}} has been expanding its manufacturing footprint, which usually puts extra pressure on quality and documentation teams.

Many manufacturers we speak with see rising deviation rates and slower release cycles when they scale, simply because batch records and approvals don’t keep up.

We’ve helped teams in similar environments reduce manual errors and shorten review times by digitizing key steps in their process.

Would it be worth a brief call to see if this is something you’re already addressing?

Best,  
{{sender_name}}
